Welcome to the University of North Texas!

UNT is a vibrant, diverse, and dynamic public university located in Denton, Texas. It's a place where innovation, creativity, and academic excellence come together.

About UNT

Founded in 1890, UNT is one of the largest universities in Texas, offering over 200 degree programs across various fields of study. The university prides itself on fostering a collaborative learning environment and its commitment to research and community service.

Why UNT?

If you’re considering UNT for your higher education, here are just a few reasons to get excited:

  • Excellent programs in arts, sciences, business, and engineering
  • A campus full of opportunities for personal growth and academic success
  • Over 400 student organizations, including clubs, Greek life, and academic societies
  • Strong alumni network and career services to help you post-graduation

Fun Fact!

UNT is home to one of the largest music schools in the country, and the university is known for its prestigious College of Music!

UNT Achievements

National Ranking in Engineering

UNT’s College of Engineering ranks among the top 100 engineering schools in the nation. It’s recognized for its innovation in research and development.

Green University

UNT has been ranked as one of the most sustainable universities in the U.S., with an emphasis on environmental responsibility and green campus initiatives.

Vibrant Campus Life

From sports events to student organizations, UNT has a campus life that will keep you active and engaged every single day!
